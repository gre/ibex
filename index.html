The Game

  • Protect and escort a group of wild ibex.
  • The world is ending, you need to progress to the right.
  • There is sleeping ibex to rescue, you will have to go deep to rescue them but be careful with the environnement!

The Gameplay

You are in god mode and you have to use the 4 elements (Air, Earth, Fire, Water) to make your animals progress safely.

Two ways of playing the game:

  • Keyboard-only:
    SPACE to draw an element, W/Z + X + C + V to switch between Air + Earth + Fire + Water.
  • Mouse-only:
    DRAG on the center cursor to draw. switch between elements with the mouse wheel (or clicking on the elements in the UI).
